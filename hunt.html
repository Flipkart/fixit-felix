The Hunt

Init
====
- Download felix.box
	* either over http (this will kill the wi-fi)
	* or from one of the CDs or pendrives hopefully floating around the room.

- At your shell:

   $ vagrant box add felix /path/to/felix.box
   $ mkdir $HOME/fixit-felix
   $ cd $HOME/fixit-felix
   $ vagrant init felix
   $ vagrant up
   $ vagrant status
   $ vagrant ssh

- Pick a team name. Say `mongrel`.
- Do join the irc channel #fixit.
- You can look at the irc archive here

Note
====
All of the commands from the glossary section of the previous page have been enabled via sudoers.
So you should be able to do, for example

   $ sudo netstat -ltpn

This would list all the listening TCP ports
